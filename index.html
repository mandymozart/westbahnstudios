The Westbahnstudios see themselves as a place where all forms of advanced music and sonic arts can be developed, rehearsed or produced.
Rental is possible on a daily basis and can be kept cost-effective through a cooperation with the City of Vienna. All work spaces were developed and designed by musicians for musicians.

For rental enquiries please contact Eva Perner by email - eva@westbahnstudios.at
